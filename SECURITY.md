## Reporting a Vulnerability

The Ivy Africa Foundation takes the security of its website, systems, and user data seriously.

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly and privately.

### How to Report

Please send details of the vulnerability to:

**Email:** [security@ivyafrica.co.za](mailto:security@ivyafrica.co.za)

Include the following information where possible:

* Description of the vulnerability
* Steps required to reproduce the issue
* Potential impact
* Screenshots, logs, or supporting evidence
* Suggested remediation (if available)

### Response Timeline

Upon receiving a vulnerability report, we will:

* Acknowledge receipt within **3 business days**
* Conduct an initial assessment within **7 business days**
* Provide status updates during the investigation process
* Notify the reporter once the issue has been resolved or otherwise addressed

### Responsible Disclosure

Please do not:

* Publicly disclose the vulnerability before it has been investigated and remediated
* Attempt to access, modify, destroy, or exfiltrate data that does not belong to you
* Conduct denial-of-service attacks or actions that may disrupt services
* Exploit vulnerabilities beyond what is necessary to demonstrate their existence

### Scope

This policy applies to:

* The Ivy Africa website
* Public web applications maintained by Ivy Africa
* Associated digital platforms under Ivy Africa management

### Out of Scope

The following are generally considered out of scope unless they present a demonstrable security impact:

* Missing security headers without an exploitable vulnerability
* Clickjacking on non-sensitive pages
* Social engineering attacks
* Denial-of-service testing
* Vulnerabilities in third-party services outside Ivy Africa's control

### Good Faith Security Research

Ivy Africa supports responsible security research conducted in good faith. Researchers who report vulnerabilities responsibly and provide sufficient information for validation will be treated respectfully throughout the disclosure process.
